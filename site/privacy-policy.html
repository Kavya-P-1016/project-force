Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 April 2026

Project Force (“we”, “us”, or “our”) respects your privacy. This policy describes the types of information we collect through projectforce.in, how we use it, and how it is protected.

Information we collect

When you submit an enquiry via our contact form, we collect your name, organisation, phone number, email address, selected service interest, and the message content. We also collect standard server log data (IP address, user agent, pages accessed) for security and analytics purposes.

How we use your information

  • Respond to your enquiry and follow up on project discussions.
  • Send relevant proposals, quotations, or service information.
  • Maintain and improve the security and performance of our website.
  • Comply with statutory and regulatory obligations applicable to our business.

Information sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information. We may share information with trusted service providers (such as email infrastructure or analytics) strictly for the purposes described above, and only under confidentiality obligations.

Data retention

Enquiry records are retained for the duration of our business relationship and as required to comply with legal or contractual obligations thereafter.

Your rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information by writing to info@projectforce.in. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

Cookies

The projectforce.in website uses only essential cookies required for core site functionality. Third-party embeds (such as Google Maps) may set their own cookies governed by the respective provider's privacy policies.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
