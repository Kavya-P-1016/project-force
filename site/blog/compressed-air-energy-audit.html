Compressed air energy audits: quick wins that pay back fast

Compressed air is often the most expensive utility in a plant. Structured audits uncover losses hidden in leaks, pressure settings, and control logic.

Priority actions

  • Leak survey by zone with repair closure targets.
  • Pressure reset after end-use validation.
  • Compressor sequencing using real demand patterns.

Measure outcomes weekly

Track kWh per unit output and compressor run-hours. Fast feedback keeps teams aligned and avoids rollback to old settings.
