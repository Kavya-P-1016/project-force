Incident investigation: choosing a root-cause method your team will use

The best method is the one your supervisors can apply consistently under real shift pressure while still producing useful corrective actions.

Match method to event complexity

  • Use 5-Why for straightforward process deviations.
  • Use fishbone when multiple systems are involved.
  • Use barrier analysis for high-consequence scenarios.

Close the loop

Every investigation should end with accountable actions, due dates, verification checks, and learnings shared across departments.
