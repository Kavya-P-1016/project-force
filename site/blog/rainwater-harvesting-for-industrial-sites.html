Rainwater harvesting for industrial sites: design mistakes to avoid

Rainwater projects fail when tanks are sized from intuition instead of roof catchment reality and process demand curves.

Three design anchors

  • Catchment mapping with runoff coefficients.
  • First-flush diversion sized for local dust conditions.
  • Storage linked to actual reuse points, not generic assumptions.

Operations matter as much as design

Simple SOPs for pre-monsoon cleaning and periodic disinfection are what keep systems productive year after year.
