RO reject water reuse strategies that are practical at plant scale

Reject streams can be useful when mapped correctly to lower-grade utility applications and supported by basic preconditioning.

Start with compatibility mapping

Identify utility points where higher TDS is acceptable and define quality envelopes to prevent corrosion or scaling surprises.

Implementation sequence

  • Characterize reject variability by shift and season.
  • Add suitable pretreatment where required.
  • Pilot one reuse loop before full-scale rollout.
