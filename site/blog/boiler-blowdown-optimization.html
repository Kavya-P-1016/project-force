Boiler blowdown optimization to save water, fuel, and downtime

Blowdown settings influence steam quality, scaling risk, and water demand. Optimizing this one control can deliver multi-utility savings.

Where savings come from

By matching blowdown rates to conductivity trends and load bands, teams avoid both excessive losses and deposit risk in heat-transfer surfaces.

Checklist for implementation

  • Validate online conductivity sensors.
  • Define load-based setpoints by shift.
  • Track makeup, fuel, and downtime together.
