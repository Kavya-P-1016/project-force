Stack emission monitoring basics for plant and utility teams

Most failures are not because analyzers are missing, but because maintenance and calibration routines are not embedded in operations.

Build a baseline first

Capture stable-load data for each stack and compare against process conditions so teams can detect drift early instead of after a notice.

Minimum operating discipline

  • Calibration schedule with ownership by shift.
  • Sensor health checks logged in daily sheets.
  • Cross-check of analyzer values with periodic manual sampling.

Audit-ready reporting

Maintain event logs for shutdowns, bypasses, and abnormal loads. Context-rich records reduce disputes during regulator review.
