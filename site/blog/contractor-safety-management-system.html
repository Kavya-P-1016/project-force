Building a contractor safety management system that actually works

Contractor incidents rarely come from policy absence. They come from weak execution in induction, supervision, and permit interfaces.

Core controls

  • Competency-based induction and role mapping.
  • Unified permit-to-work rules across all contractors.
  • Supervisor accountability with daily field verification.

Make it auditable

Track leading indicators such as closure of unsafe acts, toolbox participation quality, and permit deviations by contractor package.
